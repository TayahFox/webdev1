Misson Statement

Our mission is to give diabetics a place to have a sweet tooth without risking their health.

Company History

Founded in 1992 by Tayah Fox after being diagnosed with Type One Diabetes. Tayah Fox wanted all diabetics to have a place where their illness holds no weight on what they can and cannot eat. Our company produces diabetic freindly sweets for anyone to enjoy.
