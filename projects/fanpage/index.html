Who is BTS?

BTS is a South Korean Boy Band under BigHit Entertainment. They debuted in 2013 with their first album 2 cool 4 skool. The members consist of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.

BTS Accomplishments

In the span of their 9 year career, BTS has achieved a lot. They are curently the most streamed group on spotify, the first K-pop act to be nominated for a Grammy, and hold the record for most weeks at no.1 on Billboard's Digital Song Sales chart. Those are 3 of BTS' many accomplishments and they're still breaking records today.
